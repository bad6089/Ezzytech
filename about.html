ABOUT US

Established in 2019, we are a technology-focused company integrating R & D, production, sales and service in the field of sheet metal fabrications. We are proudly based in Selangor, Malaysia and here at our company, we are fully equipped with extensive range of manufacturing equipment and facilities that enable us to design and manufacture a broad range of metal products under one roof.

We work with all different kind of metals such as aluminium etc products can be made to order ranging across all industry sectors and for us, no job is too big or too small. Whether you need a product design or a functional and practical product for industrial use, our team will take the time to understand your requirements and turn your vision into a reality.

Sheet Metal Products!

As a company, we invest in our team so that your sheet metal fabrications are created with industry-leading expertise and care.
